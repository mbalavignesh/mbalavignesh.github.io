About Me

I’m Balavignesh Muthukaruppan—a Lead AI Architect, problem solver, artist, and photographer driven by curiosity and a constant desire to learn.

My journey in technology began in 2007 as a Product Support Engineer at Tata Consultancy Services. Early in my career, I had the opportunity to work closely with customers in Atlanta, where I managed end-to-end contact center infrastructure and workforce applications. This experience shaped my ability to understand real-world problems deeply and build solutions that actually matter.

Over the years, I transitioned from hands-on technical work into leadership roles, combining technology expertise with people management. At Verint Systems, I spent nearly a decade strengthening operational stability, mentoring teams, handling critical escalations, and building tools to reduce manual effort and improve efficiency.

In November 2025, I moved into a new chapter as a Lead AI Architect at CSC Global, where I focus on designing intelligent systems, driving AI adoption, and solving complex business challenges using modern AI technologies. My work revolves around turning ideas into scalable, impactful solutions.

I’ve been recognized with awards such as Employee of the Year (2024), Star Performer, and Customer Champion, reflecting my commitment to excellence and customer-centric thinking.

Beyond technology, I explore creativity through art, photography, and storytelling. I’ve co-authored books, contributed creative work, and continue to experiment with new forms of expression. I believe creativity and technology go hand in hand—both are about building something meaningful.

Much of my inspiration comes from my grandfather, an engineer who mentored me from a young age and continues to influence my mindset and approach to life.
